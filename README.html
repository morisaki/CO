The original source code used in our experiment was organized and restored through the courtesy of Professor Lutz Prechelt (Research Group Page) and Dr. Franz Zieris (GitHub). The original source code was used in experiments for papers published in 2001 and 2016, and we sincerely appreciate the time and effort they dedicated to its preparation and restoration. The results of our study would not have been possible without their invaluable cooperation.
